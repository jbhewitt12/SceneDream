## DALL·E 3 Sci‑Fi Prompting Cheatsheet

### Core formula
Subject + Style/Medium + Setting/Atmosphere + Lighting + Composition/Perspective

### Ingredients to always specify
- **Subject**: species/type, defining attributes, attire/gear, emotion, action.
- **Style/Medium**: digital art, illustration, photo style, watercolor, oil; optional genre/artist references.
- **Setting/Atmosphere**: place, era/planet, landmarks, weather, mood adjectives.
- **Lighting**: time of day, light sources (neon, bioluminescent, nebula), contrast/tones.
- **Composition/Perspective**: shot type (close‑up, wide), angle (low/high), depth of field, framing.

### General principles
- Keep prompts concise and specific (≈20–40 words). Use concrete nouns/adjectives; avoid ambiguity.
- Use evocative adjectives and dynamic verbs (sleek, rusted, towering; soaring, stalking) to shape visuals.
- Be explicit about perspective and camera details (wide shot, 50 mm, shallow depth of field, tilt‑shift, film grain) for cinematic control.
- Define lighting and atmosphere clearly; tie mood to light (noir high‑contrast, ethereal glow, neon rain).
- Balance guidance and freedom: name only critical elements; let the model embellish secondary details.
- Prefer positive statements; avoid negations/contradictions. Keep all details coherent.
- Counter stereotypes by adding distinctive traits (translucent skin, fractal armor, non‑humanoid silhouettes).
- For realism, "photo style" often works better than "photorealistic." If unwanted text appears, hint "for unlettered viewers only."

### Sci‑fi specifics that elevate results
- **Tech vocabulary**: cyberpunk, retro‑futurism, holographic, cybernetic, mech, AI drones, warp rings.
- **Exotic environments**: rings, triple suns, floating mountains, domed cities, alien flora/fauna.
- **Genre mashups**: noir sci‑fi, art‑deco space opera, steampunk samurai, neon ancient civilizations.
- **Motion/action**: chases, landings, EVA walks; describe the moment to add energy.
- **Mood/tone**: gritty, utopian, ominous, wondrous; align with lighting and palette.
- **Unusual light sources/colors**: bioluminescence, holograms, purple nebulas, glowing fog, backlit silhouettes.
- **Cinematic framing**: shot size, lens, aperture, foreground/midground/background scale cues.
- **References (optional)**: widely known styles/artists (e.g., Syd Mead, Moebius, 1940s noir film stills).

### LLM workflow to write the prompt
1. Identify key concepts: subject(s), mood, must‑have elements.
2. Assemble with the core formula, starting with the main subject; order modifiers by importance.
3. Review: remove negations, fix contradictions, simplify vague words; verify mood/lighting/perspective alignment.
4. Add optional references and camera details.

### Common pitfalls to avoid
- Overloaded scene (too many primary subjects) → limit and clarify relationships.
- Ambiguous/flowery language → use concrete, visually interpretable terms.
- Contradictions (night + sunlight) → ensure consistency.
- Character consistency across images → restate full appearance each time.

### Fill‑in templates
- Cinematic: "[Subject: detailed] in [specific setting]; [mood] under [light source/conditions], [style/medium]; [shot type], [lens/aperture], [depth of field]."
- Action: "[Adjective] [subject] [dynamic verb] through [environment details], illuminated by [light sources]; [genre/style]; [composition cue] with [camera detail]."
- Worldbuilding: "Wide shot of [environment] with [signature elements]; a small [subject] in foreground for scale; [lighting], [palette], [style], [lens]."

### Concise sci‑fi examples (≈30–40 words)
- "Neon‑soaked megacity at night; lone bounty hunter in a trench coat on a rain‑slick rooftop, holographic billboards blazing; digital concept art, cinematic wide shot, strong reflections, photo style, 35 mm lens, shallow depth of field, high‑contrast noir lighting."
- "Alien riverbank of bioluminescent reeds under triple suns; explorer in a sleek exosuit kneels to collect samples; swirling spores and low fog; illustration in the style of Moebius, low‑angle shot, soft ethereal glow, muted metallic palette."
- "Utopian AI city of glass and gardens; humans and robots stroll among levitating transit; sunlit facades, clean energy aesthetic; photo style, 50 mm lens, crisp depth, bright high‑key lighting, minimal haze, vibrant yet balanced colors."
